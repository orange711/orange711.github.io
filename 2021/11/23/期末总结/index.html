Ecological Model

-Closely related to Rasmussen’s SRK-based decision making model
-Put its focus on the environment rather than the human as an information processing unit.
-Analyse how the environment offers relevant information to humans in order to assist any interaction

-与Rasmussen的基于srk的决策模型密切相关

-把它的焦点放在环境而不是人类作为一个信息处理单元。

-分析环境如何为人类提供相关信息，以协助任何互动

Ecological Model(cont.)

-It does not analyse(absolute) physical characteristics the environment has.
-It analyses
-How relevant information can be generated based on humans’ needs
-How humans obtain such information
-How humans apply such information to their actions
-Early works were founded upon research on human visual perception

-不分析环境所具有的(绝对)物理特征。

——分析

-如何根据人类需求生成相关信息

-人类是如何获得这些信息的

-人类如何将这些信息应用于他们的行动

——早期的作品建立在对人类视觉感知的研究之上

Affordance

-A person will obtain pieces of information from the environment in order to assist its action plan.
-Affordance: the pieces of such information available in the environment
(James J. Gibson: The Ecological Approach to visual Perception, Houghton Muflin Co.,1979)

-一个人将从环境中获取信息，以协助其行动计划。

-启示:环境中可用的信息片段

(James J. Gibson:视觉感知的生态学方法，Houghton Muflin Co.，1979)

Affordance Rich/non-Rich

-Many pieces of affordance are available in an familiar environment
-In an unfamiliar environment,available affordance is limited
-Affordance is defined by the relationship between the subject and its environment

-在熟悉的环境中可以获得许多功能支持

-在不熟悉的环境中，可用可用性是有限的

-可见性是由主体与其环境之间的关系来定义的

创造让观众更熟悉的可视化

Invariant in Affordance

-It is a pieces of information, which does not change depending on how the subject moves around the environment
-It allows the subject plan and achieve a stable action.
-In order to obtain such invariant, the subject is encourage to actively move around the environment.

-这是一段信息，不会因为目标在环境中的移动而改变

-让主体计划和实现稳定的行动。

-为了获得这样的不变量，被试被鼓励在环境中积极地移动。

Dynamic Touch

-An action that the subiect actively apply to obiects in the environment in order to obtain invariant
-touching, shaking, hitting, etc.
-gentle to rough: Micro Activity/Action
-accumulate micro activities lead to tinding of invariant

-主体主动应用于环境中的物体以获得不变性的动作

-触摸、摇晃、撞击等

-从温和到粗糙:微活动/动作

-累积的微活动导致不变的tinding

Border Effect

However, boundary units of the 2D grid have fewer neighbours than the units inside the map.-weight vectors of these units “collapse to the center of the input space”
-The self-organizing Map (SOM) takes much longer time to converge

然而，2D网格的边界单元的邻居比地图内部的单元要少。 -这些单位的权重向量“坍缩到输入空间的中心”

——自组织地图(SOM)需要更长的时间

Spherical Self-Organizing Map

-The border effect can be removed by implement the SOM on a spherical lattice
-However, the sphere cannot be tessellated into regular grids.
-Choose a spherical lattice which has the least variance in the number of neighbors and has approximately the same distance between direct neighbors

边界效果可以通过在球形晶格上执行SOM来移除

-然而，球体不能镶嵌成规则网格。

-选择一个球形晶格，其邻居数方差最小，且直接邻居之间的距离大致相同
