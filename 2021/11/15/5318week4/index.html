5318 week4

朴素贝叶斯

Probabilistic classifiers

· Probabilistic classification methods compute the class membership probability,i.e. the probability that a given example belongs toa particular class
· Naive Bayes is a prominent example of this group
· It is based on the Bayes Theorem

$$
P(H|E) = \frac{P(E|H)证据在该假说下的概率P(H)假说的概率}{P(E)证据的概率}
$$
H是假说：hypothesis

E是证据：evidence

对于多个证据 就多次相乘 P (E1|H) * P(E2|H)同理证据的概率

· Uses the Bayes Theorem to solve classification tasks
· Two assumptions:

  1. Independence-(the values of the) attributes are conditionally independent of each other, given the class(i.e. for each class value)
  2. Equally importance-all attributes are equally important
    · Unrealistic assumptions-almost never correct
    .=>that’s why the algorithm is called Naive Bayes
    · However, these assumptions lead to a simple and easy to implement algorithm, which works surprisingly well in practice

·使用贝叶斯定理解决分类任务

·两个假设:

1)独立性-(属性的值)是有条件地相互独立的，给定类(例如。 对于每个类值)

2)同等重要——所有属性都同等重要

·不切实际的假设——几乎从不正确

.=>这就是为什么这个算法被称为朴素贝叶斯

·然而，这些假设导致了一个简单且易于实现的算法，在实践中运行得惊人地好

在遇到0概率问题的时候：

使用Laplace correction

1作为分子，m作为分母（m为属性类别的个数 ）

Missing value

忽略丢失值 只计算已知值

对于数字型的属性计算问题

高斯（必练）

Discussion

Probabilities are calculated easily due to the independence assumption Fast-requires 1 scan of the training data to calculate all statistics for both nominal and continuous attributes In many cases outperforms more sophisticated learning methods Robust to isolated noise points-such points have only negligible impact on the conditional probabilities Correlated attributes reducs the power of Naive Bayes-violation of the independence assumption
· Solution: apply feature selection beforehand to identify and discard correlated(redundant) attributes Normal distribution assumption for numeric attributes-many features are not normally distributed-solutions: Discretize the data first,i.e. numerical->nominal attributes Use other probability density functions,e.g. Poisson, binomial, gamma

由于独立假设，概率很容易计算Fast-requires 1 scan of the training data to calculate all statistics for both nominal and continuous attributes在许多情况下优于更复杂的学习方法鲁棒隔离噪声点-这些点对条件概率的影响微乎其微 相关属性降低了朴素贝叶斯违背独立性假设的能力

·解决方法:事先应用特征选择，识别并丢弃相关(冗余)属性。数值属性采用正态分布假设，很多特征不是正态分布。 使用其他概率密度函数，例如: 泊松分布、二项分布、γ
